How to Use

After installing, just use the lihelp command:

$ lihelp "find all files larger than 100MB in my home directory"

find ~ -type f -size +100M

$ lihelp "unzip archive.tar.gz and show progress"

tar -xzvf archive.tar.gz

Then, press (Enter) to run, (e) to edit, or (q) to quit.
